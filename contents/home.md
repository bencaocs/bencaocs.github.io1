

Dr. Cao Ben currently is a JSPS Fellow in UTokyo. Before this, He work as Postdoc research fellow in University of Oklahoma and is collaborating with [Prof. Yiyang Gong](https://profiles.ouhsc.edu/display/15991208) (Sloan Research Fellowships). He received his Ph.D. in Computer Science from Dalian University of Technology (DLUT), Supervised by [Prof. Qiang Zhang](http://faculty.dlut.edu.cn/zhangq/zh_CN/index/990263/list/index.htm) and [Prof. Bin Wang](http://se.dlu.edu.cn/info/1061/1341.htm), where he was jointly trained with A*STAR, Singapore, Supervised by [Prof. Yew Soon Ong](https://personal.ntu.edu.sg/asysong/home.html) (IEEE Fellow) and [Dr. Tiantian He](https://he-tiantian.github.io/). Ben research focuses on the intersection of computer science and biotechnology (AI4Sci), with a commitment to solving biological problems using computational methods. His work spans areas such as DNA data storage and security, protein engineer, gene expression, Deep learning, and large language models (LLM). Dr. Cao has published over 20 papers as the first or corresponding author in international journals and conference, such as Cell Reports, Cell Reports Methods, IEEE Transactions, AAAI, and BIB, including one ESI hot paper and two ESI highly cited papers. He actively contributes to the academic community, serving as the Associate Editor of the Journal of Artificial Intelligence in Bioinformatics, and has been invited to review for ~30 SCI journals and Conference. He has received the National Scholarship twice.
